Once upon a time, there was a big, scary monster. The monster lived in a dark cave. He was very scary, but he wanted to make friends. One day, he went to the cave to find friends.
In the cave, the monster met a little girl. The girl was not scared of the monster. She said, "Hi, monster! Do you want to play with me?" The monster was scared of the monster and ran away. The girl and the monster became best friends.
But then, something unexpected happened. The monster started to grow bigger and bigger. Soon, he was as big as the girl! The monster was surprised but happy. Now, the monster was not scary anymore. The monster and the girl became best friends and played together in the cave.
