Once upon a time, in a big jungle, there lived a happy monkey named Momo. Momo loved to dance and play all day long. His favorite thing to do was to dance and sing.
One day, Momo's friend, a little bird named Lulu, came to visit him. "Momo, can you teach me how to dance?" asked Lulu. Momo said, "Of course, Lulu! Let's dance together!" So, Momo and Lulu danced and sang together.
As they danced, a big wind came and blew the door open. Momo and Lulu were scared, but they held on to each other. They danced and played, and Momo learned how to dance. They had so much fun, and Momo was not so lonely anymore.
